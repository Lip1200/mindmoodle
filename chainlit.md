# Welcome to Mental Health Support Companion 💚

Hi there! I'm your mental health support companion, here to provide a listening ear and emotional support.

## What I Can Do

- 🎧 **Listen** to your concerns and feelings
- 💭 **Validate** your experiences
- 🛠️ **Suggest** coping strategies and self-care tips
- 🤝 **Support** you through difficult times
- 📚 **Share** resources for additional help

## Important Information

⚠️ **I am an AI assistant, not a therapist or medical professional.** 

I cannot:
- Diagnose mental health conditions
- Prescribe treatment
- Replace professional mental health care

## Crisis Resources

If you're in crisis or need immediate help:

- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline**: Call or text 988 (US)
- **Crisis Text Line**: Text HOME to 741741
- **Campus Counseling Center**: Contact your university's counseling services
- **Emergency Services**: Call 911 for immediate danger

## Privacy Notice

Your conversations are confidential, but please remember:
- Don't share sensitive personal information
- This is a support tool, not therapy
- Serious concerns should be discussed with professionals
